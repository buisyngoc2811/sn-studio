\# SN Studio Design System



Style:

\- Dark premium SaaS

\- Inspired by Linear, Vercel, Raycast

\- Red accent

\- Glass cards

\- Soft glow

\- Sharp but clean layout



Rules:

\- Do not redesign randomly

\- Keep current layout

\- Improve spacing, typography, hover, empty states, loading states

\- Keep UI consistent across Home, Apps, Marketplace, Dashboard, Admin



Colors:

\- Background: near black

\- Surface: dark gray / glass

\- Accent: red / pink red

\- Success: green

\- Warning: amber

\- Error: red



Components:

\- Buttons must feel premium

\- Cards need consistent padding and radius

\- Tables must be readable

\- Admin pages must look professional

\- Marketplace cards must look like a real product store



Motion:

\- Subtle only

\- No childish animation

\- No excessive effects
